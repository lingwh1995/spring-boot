1.查看git相关信息(branch、commit)
    1>.pom增加
        <plugin>
            <groupId>pl.project13.maven</groupId>
            <artifactId>git-commit-id-plugin</artifactId>
            <version>2.1.15</version>
            <executions>
                <execution>
                    <goals>
                        <goal>revision</goal>
                    </goals>
                </execution>
            </executions>
            <configuration>
                <dotGitDirectory>${project.basedir}/.git</dotGitDirectory>
            </configuration>
        </plugin>
    2>.YML中增加git相关配置
        management:
          info:
            git:
              mode: full
    3>.生成git.properties
        idea右侧manven面板->spring-boot-actuator
            ->Plugins->git-commit-id->
                点击 git-commit-id:revision
    4>.启动项目
        访问:
            http://localhost:8080/actuator/info
        查看其中git相关信息(branch、commit)
    5>.特别注意:
        要先使用插件生成git.properties访问才能查看到git相关信息(branch、commit),否则无法查看到git相关信息
